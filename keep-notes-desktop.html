Keep Notes

Version 3.5

Overview

Keep Notes is an app built to store your notes, thoughts and ideas. It hopes to provide a clean experience while also adding useful functionalities that you'll use.

Changenotes

  • With this update, note colors have been introduced. You can choose from purple, white, salmon, yellow, blue and green.
  • You can now perform quick calculations when creating notes and add the answers to your note when you're finished.
  • Ghost mode is now available, allowing you to see the contents of your notes.
  • You can now see the details of a note in an expanded view.

Keep Notes

Version 3.0

Changenotes

  • With this update, the UI has seen the biggest redesign yet. The Keep Notes App now has a a more modern, elegant look with an emphasis on rounded corners and shading. The app now meets the Material Design guidelines.
  • Introduces a new, extensive "Dark Mode".
  • To edit a note, you now have to double-click the note.
